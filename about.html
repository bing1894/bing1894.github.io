About Us

Ambon Blue Rose Divers

Ambon Blue Rose Divers is a small and experienced dive shop which has operated from 2006. The owners are local people: Agustinus and Bambang, they dived in this area for more than 10years and has good relationship with the local village. Our super divemaster Benio is an ambon frogfish lover, he is experienced and loves diving a lot, finding some special creatures in ambon is his hobby.

Ambon Blue Rose Divers is located in Laha village, Ambon Bay, Central Maluku, East of Indonesia. 10 minutes away from Ambon air port by taxi. We have 3 simple and clean rooms in the dive shop with hot shower, air-condition, and WIFI only for our diving customers. Ambon Blue Rose Divers has 2 speed boats for diving everyday, and it takes 1-5 minutes to the muck diving sites in Ambon bay from our dive shop. Ambon bay is still a quiet and outstanding area for muck diving.

We provide Banda islands diving package trip as well.
